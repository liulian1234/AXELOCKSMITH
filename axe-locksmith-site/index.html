What to do when your key breaks inside the lock

Do not force the lock. Take a clear photo and ask for advice before trying to pull it out.

When should you replace your door lock?

Replace it when the key turns roughly, the lock is loose, or after moving into a new unit.

Digital lock battery warning signs

Slow response, repeated beeping and weak display can be early signs of battery issues.
